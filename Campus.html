ST.Georges College

Campus Discription

St. George's College, Agra, is one of the oldest convent college in India. It is a Minority Anglo-Indian Christian Institution granted Minority Rights under Article 30 of the Indian Constitution. It is located near Mall Road and near to Targhar. The campus is approximately 40 acres (160,000 m2).The students are widely named as Georgians in the city. The college is accredited under AKTU. The college was established in 1998 as an Army college for students at Agra. With the passing by of time it started admitting students from civilian background as well.
